Remote sensing imagery

Remote sensing is the process of detecting and monitoring the physical characteristics of an area by measuring its reflected and emitted radiation at a distance.
NordMap offer a comprehensive range of remote sensing, photogrammetry and radar services that will assist your organisation in leveraging additional benefits from satellite and aerial imagery.

A vector map, i a database of points, lines and polygons which collectively make up all the features on the map.
